The Last of Us - Part 1

**The Last of Us Part I** is a gripping, emotionally charged action-adventure set in a post-apocalyptic world ravaged by a deadly fungal infection. Play as Joel, a hardened survivor, and Ellie, a young girl with a mysterious immunity, as they journey through a decaying, hostile America. Face terrifying infected creatures and ruthless human enemies while navigating stunning, ruined landscapes. Experience a deeply personal story of survival, sacrifice, and the unbreakable bond between the two characters, all brought to life with breathtaking visuals and heart-pounding gameplay.

Developer: Naughty Dogs

Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
