About Us

Game Master is a company that was founded in 2025 by two software engineering students- Eran and Matan. The company was founded with the goal of providing a platform for gamers to purchase games and consoles at the best prices. We run on the belief that gaming should be accessible to everyone, and we strive to make that a reality by offering a wide range of products at affordable prices. Our team is dedicated to providing the best customer service and ensuring that our customers have a positive experience when shopping with us. We are constantly working to improve our services and expand our product offerings to better serve our customers. Thank you for choosing Game Master, and we look forward to serving you for many years to come!
