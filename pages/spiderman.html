Spiderman 2

**Spider-Man 2** for the PS5 is an electrifying open-world adventure that lets you step into the shoes of both Peter Parker and Miles Morales. As the city faces an unprecedented threat, you'll swing through a richly detailed New York, taking on formidable villains like Venom and Kraven the Hunter. Utilize unique abilities from both Spider-Men, from Peter’s advanced suit tech to Miles’ electrifying powers, to battle enemies, solve puzzles, and protect the city. With fast-paced combat, stunning visuals, and an emotional narrative, *Spider-Man 2* delivers the ultimate superhero experience.

Developer: Insomniac Games

Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
