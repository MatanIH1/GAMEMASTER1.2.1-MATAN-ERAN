God of War - Ragnarök

**God of War Ragnarök** is an epic action-adventure that takes Kratos and Atreus on a mythic journey through the Nine Realms. Face powerful Norse gods, deadly creatures, and world-ending threats in brutal, cinematic combat. Wield the Leviathan Axe and Blades of Chaos, mastering new abilities and combos. Explore stunning landscapes, solve intricate puzzles, and uncover the fate of the realms as Ragnarök approaches. Experience a gripping story of destiny, fatherhood, and survival in this breathtaking Norse saga.
