God of War: Ragnarök

God of War: Ragnarök is an epic action-adventure game that follows Kratos and Atreus as they journey through the Nine Realms to prevent the prophesied end of the world. Facing fierce Norse gods, mythical creatures, and treacherous landscapes, Kratos must embrace his past while guiding Atreus in uncovering his true destiny. With brutal combat, breathtaking visuals, and an emotionally charged story, Ragnarök delivers the ultimate God of War experience.

Developer: Santa Monica Studio

Publisher: PlayStation Studios
